Dumpling Soup

Description

Dumpling soup is one of my favourite go-to drunken midnight snacks!

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles (spicy preferred)
  • Frozen dumplings

That's it!

Method

  1. Boil water
  2. Add dumplings
  3. After about 5 minutes, add noodles and stock
  4. Cook for 2-3 more minutes
  5. Serve!

Yum!

Eggs on Toast

Description

Eggs on toast is a quick and easy breakfast or yummy snack

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs

Optional Extras:

  • Butter
  • Sauce of your choice
  • Avocado (if you never want a roof over your head)

Method

  1. Toast bread
  2. Cook eggs your preferred way (I like fried or scrambled)
  3. Place eggs on toast

Yum!

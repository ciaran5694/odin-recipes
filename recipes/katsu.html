Katsu Curry

Description

Katsu curry is the most complex dish on this website, so prepare yourself for some Masterchef level shit!

Ingredients

  • Katsu curry base
  • Chicken schnitzel
  • 1 Potato
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • Steamed rice

Method

This recipe assumes you have already cooked your schnitzel and rice. If you haven't go find your own recipe. I am a basic HTML website, not a cooking blog with the authors life story and links to a billion other recipes.

  1. Chop and stir-fry veg for five minutes
  2. Add water to cover then simmer for about 15 minutes
  3. Add katsu base and stir gently and constantly until dissolved
  4. Cook for 2-3 more minutes
  5. You can adjust the consistency to make it thinner by adding more water or thicker by reducing based on your preference
